# Product

Celis is a Somalia P2P marketplace. The full product source is `somalia-p2p-marketplace-prd.md`.

## Users

- Buyers browsing and purchasing from listings.
- Sellers creating listings and managing seller packages.
- Admin and moderation staff managing listings, users, orders, payouts, reports, roles, and platform settings.
- Platform operators responsible for auditability, marketplace safety, and financial correctness.

## Product Principles

1. **Trust is the product.** Marketplace actions must be transparent and reversible where possible.
2. **Safety before speed.** Moderation, RBAC, payments, payouts, and destructive actions need confirmation and auditability.
3. **Performance at scale.** Search, browse, admin tables, and reports must not load all rows into the UI.
4. **Seller clarity.** Listing creation, packages, expiry, and payment steps must be explicit.
5. **Admin accountability.** Sensitive admin decisions should be traceable through audit logs.
